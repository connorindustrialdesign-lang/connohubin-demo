About Me

Industrial designer and maker passionate about creating thoughtful, functional objects that solve real problems.

Resume CV Letters

With a background in industrial design and engineering, I combine technical problem-solving with user-centered design thinking. My work spans from concept sketches and 3D modeling to fabrication and final prototyping, ensuring every project is grounded in both aesthetics and functionality.

I'm driven by the challenge of simplifying complex problems through design, whether creating tools for professionals, consumer products, or art installations. I believe great design is invisible—it works so well that users never think about how it was made.

When I'm not designing, you can find me in the shop building prototypes, exploring new fabrication techniques, or drawing inspiration from everyday objects.
