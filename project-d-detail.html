Brij

bridging the gap between senior citizens and technology

Problem

Technology evolves faster than people. When people reach a certain age they no longer feel a need to learn the trending tech. In fact, they would much rather loose functionality in order to preserve the user experience of their youth. Brij aim is to help users of this demographic to connect with friends and family in a smartphone age.
