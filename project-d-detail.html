Brij

bridging the gap between senior citizens and technology

Problem

Technology evolves faster than people. Often when someone reaches a certain age they no longer feel a need to learn or understand the current trending tech. In some cases they would loss of functionality in order to preserve the user experience of a familiar format.

Brij's aim is to aid users who fall into this catagory an ability to connect with friends and family.

heads-up display

The heads-up display allows users to print out any email or text message correspondence. Users can also see the email they are drafting in real-time through the heads-up display, making it easy to compose messages on the familiar typewriter form factor.

Final Product

The final sequence combines hero renders and production-facing views to show form, printability, and the heads-up display behavior in context.
