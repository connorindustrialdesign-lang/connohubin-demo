This progression from technical work to engineering oversight strengthened my ability to translate complex requirements into actionable design solutions and deepened my understanding of how engineered products scale from prototype to full-scale manufacturing.

About Shope Concrete Products

Shope Concrete Products is a Western Washington manufacturer and wholesale distributor of precast concrete pipe, manholes, catch basins, and other related concrete products. They provide local, state, federal, and regional contractors products and solutions for underground infrastructures in residential and urban communities, businesses, streets, roads, and freeways.

Founded in 1920, Shope Concrete has stood the test of time with over 100 years of experience in the precast concrete industry, growing 320% between 1997 and 2006.
