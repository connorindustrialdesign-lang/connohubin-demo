Project F

A redesigned nightstand that combines organization with integrated lighting and charging.

The Problem

OCR text from research boards reveals the core insight: nightstands become cluttered and unorganized spaces, yet they're frequently accessed for essential items at bedtime.

The goal was to create a bedside platform that transforms this chaos into a thoughtfully organized, integrated solution.

Research & Insight

Research emphasized that everyday objects—phone, wallet, keys, pen—clutter the bedside. A designed solution must accommodate all of these while remaining beautiful and intentional.

The insight is that this frequently-used furnishing needs careful consideration of both spatial organization and material finishes.

Design Iterations

Formal exploration covered structural approaches, leg details, platform proportions, lighting positioning, and electrical integration into the bedside form.

Each iteration refined the relationship between storage zones, device charging, and integrated illumination.

Final Product

The resolved design shows the nightstand in use contexts: lights out mode for bedtime, and lights on mode for functionality and wayfinding during the night.
